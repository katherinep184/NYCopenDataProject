Around 140 million babies are born every year in the world. That's more than four births every second of every day. More than half of all births take place in Asia with 25 million births in India and 16 million births in China alone every year.In 2023, there were approximately 3.6 million babies born in the United States.

In 2023, an estimated 3,667,758 babies were born in the United States, which translates to approximately 9,852 births per day. The total number of births has declined since the most recent high in 2007, with the general fertility rate also showing a decrease. In 2023, India had the most births per day, accounting for 63,600 births. This is more than twice the number of births per day in China, which was in second place. A Visual Capitalist report estimated that India saw over 2,600 births every hour in 2023. India, China, and Nigeria accounted for nearly 30% of global daily births in 2023.
